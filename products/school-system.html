Real-Time Insights

Dashboard Overview

Real-time overview of school operations — student counts, attendance, admissions, and fee status. Quick access to key metrics and visual reports for informed decisions at a glance.

Multi-Campus

Campus & Shift Management

Manage multiple campuses within a single system and support different shifts (morning/evening) seamlessly. Administrators can standardize processes across branches while maintaining separate reporting and operations for each campus.

Admissions Upcoming

Student Enrollment & Admissions

Simplify the entire admission cycle, from online application to registration and seat allocation. Parents can complete forms and payments online, while administrators can track inquiries, approvals, and confirmations in real-time.

Centralized Data

Student Information System (SIS)

Store and manage comprehensive student records including demographics, medical history, academic progress, and family details. All data is centralized and easily accessible to authorized staff.

Student page also allows adding multiple parents and guardians.

Discounts & Scholarships

Fee Discount Package

Create customized discount schemes by defining which fee types should have specific discounts. Apply as fixed amount or percentage. Assign packages directly to students — the system automatically adjusts invoices.

✓ Transparent, consistent, error-free process

Class-Wise Fees

Fee Structure

Define class-wise fee structures by setting applicable fee heads and amounts for each class. Structures are applied based on effective from–to dates, and the system automatically generates accurate fee invoices for all enrolled students during that period.

Organization

Classroom & Section Management

Create and manage classrooms, sections, and teacher assignments efficiently. Ensure students are enrolled in the correct classes and teachers have the tools they need to manage their groups effectively.

Tracking

Attendance Management

Track daily attendance through web or mobile entry. Automated alerts and notifications keep parents informed of absences, while administrators can analyze attendance patterns for performance insights.

Financial Control

Fee & Finance Management

Automate fee invoicing at school, class, or student level with flexibility for discounts, partial payments, and corrections. Generate detailed financial reports such as fee collection, defaulters, and pending balances.

Search & Filter Vouchers

Quickly search and filter student fee vouchers by class, classroom, student, and month. Display net receivable, arrears, total amount, and pending balances.

Voucher Operations

  • View & Edit Voucher Amounts
  • Move Fee Types to next month (with reason)
  • Print Vouchers (individual or bulk)
  • Process Payments (full or partial)
  • Apply Discounts (fixed or percentage)
  • Extend Expiry Dates
Bulk Upload

Import Payment in Bulk

Process registration fee payments in bulk by uploading a bank-provided Excel file (.xlsx or .xls). Hundreds of fee payments can be recorded in a single operation instead of entering them one by one.

Scheduling

Timetable & Scheduling

Design timetables quickly using user-friendly templates. Prevent scheduling conflicts, manage substitutions, and optimize classroom utilization for smooth academic operations.

Efficiency

Bulk Operations

Save time by uploading large sets of student data, fee structures, and vouchers in one go. Reduces manual entry efforts and ensures accuracy, consistency, and faster onboarding of information.
